How we Got HERE

Each day we strive, grind, and work hard. Together we have formed, create, and build this website day by day until the very last hour. The only thing that is keeping us sane is the comfort of one another.

Our Mission

Our site aims to be ground zero for spreading awareness against malicious attacks that are prevalent on the internet. We aim to be proponents of cyberspace vigilance to aid in limiting the damage cyberattacks can inflict on users. By collectively strengthening our knowledge regarding the cyberspace and its dangers, we make the digital space we often use a safer place for everyone to enjoy.
